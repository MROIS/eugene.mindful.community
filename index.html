Still Water Sangha is a Buddhist meditation group in the Zen tradition of Thich Nhat Hanh located in Eugene Oregon.

We meet bi-weekly for silent and walking meditation, dharma talks, dharma discussion.

Who is Thich Nhat Hanh?

Zen Master Thich Nhat Hanh is a global spiritual leader, poet and peace activist, revered throughout the world for his powerful teachings and bestselling writings on mindfulness and peace.
