Still Water Sangha is a Buddhist meditation group in the Zen tradition of Thich Nhat Hanh located in Eugene Oregon.

We meet bi-monthly for silent and walking meditation, dharma talks, dharma discussion.

Who is Thich Nhat Hanh?

Zen Master Thich Nhat Hanh is a global spiritual leader, poet and peace activist, revered throughout the world for his powerful teachings and bestselling writings on mindfulness and peace. His key teaching is that, through mindfulness, we can learn to live happily in the present moment. ...Read More
